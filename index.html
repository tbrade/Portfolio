About Me

Hi my name is Taylor Braden. I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Geospatial Technologies at the University of Washington. I currently hold a certificate in Geographic Information Systems as well as a B.S. in Environmental Science from the University of Washington. I have also attained a certificate in Restoration Ecology as well. Two communications classes and a Managing Organizations class have helped to enhance my project management skills. My ideal job would be a job where I can apply my GIS skills to help preserve habitat for endangered species.
